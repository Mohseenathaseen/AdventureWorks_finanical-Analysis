# AdventureWorks_finanical-Analysis

# Report Overview

This Financial Analysis dashboard provides a complete view of the organization's financial performance by monitoring key metrics such as **Revenue, Expenses, Profit & Loss, Accounts Receivable (AR), Accounts Payable (AP), Cash Flow, and Balance Sheet**. It enables finance teams and business stakeholders to track financial performance over different reporting periods using interactive filters and slicers.

The dashboard presents key financial KPIs through intuitive visualizations, allowing users to monitor revenue trends, profitability, operating expenses, budget versus actual performance, and project financials. Users can also analyze receivables and payables, identify outstanding balances, and gain insights into cash flow and overall financial health.

## Special Features

* **Interactive Navigation Panel** – A user-friendly navigation menu enables quick access to different financial reports, providing a seamless reporting experience.

* **Drill-Through Analysis** – Users can drill through summary-level financial metrics to view detailed transaction information, project-level costs, customer invoices, vendor payments, and account-level data for deeper analysis.

* **AI-Powered Financial Insights** – Utilizes **Key Influencers** and **Decomposition Tree** visuals to identify the primary factors affecting revenue, profitability, expenses, and cash flow, helping finance teams make informed business decisions.

* **Dynamic Time Intelligence** – Supports Month-to-Date (MTD), Quarter-to-Date (QTD), Year-to-Date (YTD), and custom date range analysis to compare financial performance across different periods.

* **Financial KPI Monitoring** – Tracks critical metrics including Revenue, Gross Profit, Net Profit, EBITDA, Operating Margin, AR Aging, AP Aging, Budget vs. Actual, and Cash Flow to support strategic financial planning.
